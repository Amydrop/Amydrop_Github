Our Story

Born from love

Kuppusamy

Amydrop was born from a father's dedication and passion. For 5 years, Kuppusamy perfected a liquid detergent formula that his family, friends, and neighbours trusted and loved.

Today, his legacy lives on — bottled with the same love, care, and quality in every drop of Amydrop. The brand name itself carries his memory — Amy from Kuppusamy, and drop for every precious drop of his dedication.

Every bottle of Amydrop carries his love, his passion, and his life's work — continuing through us.
